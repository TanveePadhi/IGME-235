Description

Speakeasy is a board game set in the 1920's during Prohibition in Manhattan. A 1-4 player work-placement and card-management game you are a mobster who is running their own speakeasy. Use your resources wisely and grow your business. Become the most wealthy and powerful mobster in the city. Hire goons. Work with others. Commit robbery. Fill your pockets with free tax money.
